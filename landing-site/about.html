Our Story

Born from a deep understanding of Africa's unique healthcare challenges

Medikal Africa was founded with a clear vision: to address the critical challenge of antimicrobial resistance (AMR) that threatens healthcare systems across the African continent. Our journey began when our founder, Olivier Niyonshima, recognized the urgent need for intelligent, accessible healthcare solutions tailored specifically for African contexts.

Unlike generic healthcare platforms, Medikal is built from the ground up with African healthcare systems in mind. We understand the unique challenges faced by clinicians and patients across 54 diverse countries, from language barriers to resource constraints, and we've designed our platform to address these realities.

Through strategic partnerships with organizations like the AMR Initiative Rwanda, Africa CDC, and leading technology companies, we're creating a comprehensive ecosystem that supports healthcare providers, educates patients, and monitors antimicrobial resistance patterns in real-time.
