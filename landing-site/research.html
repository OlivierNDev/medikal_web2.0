Mission & Impact

Strengthening Health Systems Across Africa

Our Mission

A 70% Reduction in AMR by 2030

Medikal Africa was founded with a clear vision: to address the critical challenge of antimicrobial resistance threatening healthcare systems across the continent.

We combine cutting-edge AI technology with deep local understanding to build clinical intelligence infrastructure that serves all 54 African countries and sets a new global standard for AMR combat.
