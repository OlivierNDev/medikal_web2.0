What is Medikal?

Medikal is an AI-powered platform developed to assist African clinicians and patients in fighting antimicrobial resistance, improving diagnosis, guiding safe medication use, and preserving life-saving drugs for future generations.

AI Diagnosis

Advanced AI algorithms assist healthcare providers in accurate diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

AMR Protection

Intelligent monitoring and prevention of antimicrobial resistance to preserve medication effectiveness.

Africa-Centered

Built specifically for African healthcare systems, addressing unique challenges and cultural contexts.

What We're Tackling

Antimicrobial Resistance is one of the biggest threats to global health. In Africa, we're fighting back with AI.

Antibiotic Overuse

Over-prescription and misuse of antibiotics leading to resistance
