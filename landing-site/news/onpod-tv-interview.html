Leadership Spotlight: ONPOD TV, Canada

Our founder and CEO, Olivier Niyonshima, was featured on ONPOD TV Canada in an exclusive interview highlighting Medikal's groundbreaking mission to fight antimicrobial resistance (AMR) across Africa through artificial intelligence.

Shaping the Future of African Healthcare with AI

As the visionary leader behind ZeroX Intelligence and Medikal Africa, Olivier shared how AI can transform healthcare delivery, enhance diagnostics, optimize treatment, and reduce AMR by up to 70%.

He emphasized the importance of African-led innovation, collaboration, and responsible AI use in building resilient health systems and ensuring equitable access to effective medicines across the continent.

Watch the Full Interview

Key Discussion Points

During the interview, Olivier discussed the critical role of artificial intelligence in addressing one of Africa's most pressing healthcare challenges: antimicrobial resistance. He outlined how Medikal's AI-powered platform provides clinicians with evidence-based prescribing guidance, resistance pattern detection, and real-time decision support.

The conversation highlighted the importance of local context in healthcare technology development, with Olivier emphasizing that African solutions must be built by Africans who understand the unique challenges, cultural contexts, and resource constraints of the continent's healthcare systems.

He also spoke about the collaborative nature of this work, acknowledging partnerships with organizations like AMR Initiative Rwanda and the broader commitment to ensuring that AI benefits all communities, not just those in well-resourced regions.
