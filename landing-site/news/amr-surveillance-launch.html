Medikal Launches AI-Powered AMR Surveillance System

Revolutionizing Antimicrobial Resistance Monitoring

Medikal introduces groundbreaking AI technology to combat antimicrobial resistance across East African healthcare facilities. This new surveillance system represents a significant advancement in real-time AMR monitoring and prevention.

The AI-powered platform enables healthcare providers to track resistance patterns, optimize antibiotic prescribing, and implement evidence-based treatment protocols that help preserve the effectiveness of critical medications.
