Partnership with AMR Initiative Rwanda Expands

Strengthening Collaboration for Greater Impact

Medikal strengthens collaboration with AMR Initiative Rwanda to reach more healthcare facilities across the region. This expanded partnership enables broader deployment of AI-powered healthcare solutions throughout East Africa.

Together, we're working to build resilient healthcare systems that can effectively combat antimicrobial resistance and improve patient outcomes across the continent.
