Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Medikal platform, features, and support

General Questions
What is Medikal platform?

Medikal is an AI-powered healthcare platform designed specifically for African healthcare challenges. It combines machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing to provide intelligent medical assistance, combat antimicrobial resistance (AMR), and deliver healthcare solutions across desktop, mobile, SMS, and WhatsApp platforms in multiple languages including Kinyarwanda, English, and French.

Who developed Medikal?

Medikal is developed through a partnership between ZeroX Intelligence (a Rwandan-Canadian AI company) and the AMR Initiative Rwanda. This collaboration combines advanced AI technology expertise with deep understanding of African healthcare challenges and antimicrobial resistance issues.

What languages does Medikal support?

Medikal supports three languages to serve diverse African communities:

  • Kinyarwanda - Native language support for local communities
  • English - International communication and medical documentation
  • French - Regional healthcare integration across francophone Africa
What is the main goal of Medikal?

Medikal's primary goal is to achieve a 70% reduction in antimicrobial resistance (AMR) cases across Africa while transforming healthcare delivery through AI-powered solutions. The platform aims to provide accessible, intelligent healthcare support that bridges the gap between advanced medical technology and African healthcare needs.

Technical Questions
What platforms is Medikal available on?

Medikal is designed for multi-platform accessibility to reach all communities:

  • Desktop Applications - Windows, macOS, and Linux versions for offline access
  • Mobile Apps - Android and iOS applications for patients and healthcare workers
  • SMS Integration - Text message support for medication reminders and basic consultations
  • WhatsApp - Appointment notifications and health alerts
  • Web Platform - Browser-based access for universal compatibility
What AI technologies does Medikal use?

Medikal leverages cutting-edge AI technologies:

  • Machine Learning - 94-95% diagnostic accuracy with real-time analysis
  • Deep Learning - Neural networks for complex medical pattern recognition
  • Natural Language Processing - Multilingual medical communication and understanding
  • Computer Vision - Medical image analysis and diagnostic support
  • Predictive Analytics - AMR risk assessment and treatment optimization
How accurate is Medikal's AI diagnostic system?

Medikal's AI diagnostic system achieves 94-95% accuracy in medical analysis and recommendations. The system processes information in real-time and continuously learns from medical data to improve its accuracy. However, it's important to note that Medikal is designed to assist healthcare professionals, not replace clinical judgment.

Can Medikal work offline?

Yes, Medikal desktop applications are designed to work offline, making it accessible in remote areas with limited internet connectivity. Core diagnostic features and medical reference materials are available locally, with optional cloud synchronization when internet access is available for updates and cross-platform medical record access.

Medical & Health Questions
How does Medikal combat antimicrobial resistance (AMR)?

Medikal combats AMR through multiple AI-powered approaches:

  • WHO AWaRe Classification - Integration with international antibiotic guidelines
  • Real-time Resistance Detection - Instant analysis of resistance patterns
  • Smart Prescriptions - AI-guided antibiotic recommendations
  • Predictive Analytics - Forecasting resistance trends and outbreaks
  • Cross-Africa Data - Shared medical records network for resistance tracking
Can Medikal replace my doctor?

No, Medikal is not a replacement for professional medical care. Medikal is designed as an AI assistant to support healthcare professionals and provide preliminary guidance to patients. It enhances clinical decision-making but should never replace professional medical consultation, especially for serious health concerns or emergency situations. Always consult qualified healthcare providers for medical diagnoses and treatment decisions.

What types of medical conditions can Medikal help with?

Medikal provides AI assistance across various healthcare areas:

  • Infectious Diseases - Bacterial, viral, and parasitic infections with AMR considerations
  • Symptom Analysis - Initial assessment and differential diagnosis suggestions
  • Medical Imaging - Analysis and interpretation support for diagnostic images
  • Medication Management - Drug interactions, dosing, and resistance-aware prescribing
  • Preventive Care - Health screening recommendations and risk assessments
Is my medical data secure with Medikal?

Yes, Medikal implements comprehensive healthcare data protection measures including HIPAA compliance standards, end-to-end encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and strict access controls. Medical data is anonymized for AI training purposes, and users maintain full control over their health information. The platform follows international healthcare data protection regulations and African data sovereignty requirements.
