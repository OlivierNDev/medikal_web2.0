Challenges We Address

Understanding the critical healthcare challenges in Africa that drive our innovation

Antibiotic Overuse

Over-prescription and misuse of antibiotics leading to antimicrobial resistance, one of the biggest threats to global health.

Critical Issue

Misdiagnosis

Lack of advanced diagnostic tools leading to incorrect treatment decisions and delayed patient care in underserved communities.

High Priority

Information Gap

Limited access to updated medical information, clinical guidelines, and evidence-based treatment protocols across African healthcare systems.
